How to use Join

Add a new task by selecting "Add Task"
1. Fill in the task name on "Title".
2. Click on calendar icon and select the due date.
3. Select the task category and urgency by clicking on the respective fields.
4. Fill in the description, so the users know exactly what to do.
5. At "assigned to" you can choose other users who should work on that task. Just click on the plus and then you can select available users by clicking on their photo.
6. Finish creating a new task by touching the "Create Task"-Button.

You can find all created tasks at "Backlog".
When you click on "Board", you can see all the tasks you´re assigned to and their status.
First the status is always "to do" and when you´re still working on that task you can pull it in the status you need. You can move them when touching the arrows or catch the task-field with your mouse and drag it to the new status (drag & drop only works on desktop version). When you click on the garbage can you can delete a task. But if you delete something it´s irreversible.
